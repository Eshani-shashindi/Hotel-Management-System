About Us

Hotel Elegance is a famous and well-reputed hotel in Sri Lanka that provides a wide variety of recreational activities for guests to enjoy.

Our hotel is designed according to the enduring vision of an innovative entrepreneur Albert Perera, with the expectation of giving their customers unforgettable and delicious experiences during their stay.

We provides various themes, from 1980's vintage and aesthetic themes to 2024's trending themes. The guests can customize their rooms and cuisine according to their preferred themes.

Further, there is a pool, a gym and a karaoke bar for customers to enjoy free of charge.
